**How Does Mapping United Work?**

📊 Our app uses advanced technology to provide real-time information on available parking spots in your area. Simply download the app from our website (https://www.mapping-united.com), enter your location, and voilà! You'll have access to a map showing you the nearest parking options, including prices, availability, and distances.
